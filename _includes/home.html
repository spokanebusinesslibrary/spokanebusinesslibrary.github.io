About the Business Library

The Spokane Business Library is a collaboration between Spokane Public Library (SPL) and the Spokane Country Library District (SCLD).

In developing a single county-wide platform featuring SCLD and SPL business resources, our goal is to provide all Spokane County residents with greater access to the business resources currently offered by the Spokane County Library District and Spokane Public Library.

To access these services you will need a valid library card at either SPL or SCLD.

Using the Business Library

Spokane Business Library is a collaboration between the Spokane Country Library District (SCLD) and Spokane Public Library (SPL).

To access these services you will need a valid library card at either SPL or SCLD.

  • SCLD Country barcodes begin with 509.
  • SPL City barcodes begin with 27413.

First, identify your library card. Then follow the links labeled for SCLD Country or SPL City users. That's it!
