Business Library Resources

The Spokane Business Library is a collaboration between Spokane Public Library (SPL) and the Spokane Country Library District (SCLD). To access these services you will need a valid library card at either SPL or SCLD.

SPL barcodes begin with 27413.

SCLD barcodes begin with 509.

Once you have identified your library card, simply following the appropriate links labeled for City and County users.
